Lone Star Puzzles — Data Deletion Instructions

What Data Does Lone Star Store?

Lone Star stores all gameplay data locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Puzzle progress and completion status
  • Game settings and preferences (sound, haptics, theme)
  • Statistics (solve times, streaks, hint usage)

We do not store any data on external servers. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no personal information transmitted from your device.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Reset Progress In-App

Open Lone Star > Settings > Reset All Progress. This clears all saved puzzle completions, statistics, and streaks. This action cannot be undone.

Option 2: Clear App Data (Android)

Go to your device Settings > Apps > Lone Star > Storage > Clear Data. This removes all locally stored app data and resets the app to its initial state.

Option 3: Clear App Data (iOS)

On iOS, go to Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Lone Star > Delete App, then reinstall from the App Store. Alternatively, use the in-app Reset option described above.

Option 4: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Lone Star from your device permanently removes all locally stored data. No data remains on any server after uninstallation.

Third-Party Data

The free version of Lone Star uses Google AdMob for advertising. Data collected by AdMob (such as your Advertising ID) is managed by Google. To manage or delete this data:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Delete advertising ID"
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > disable tracking for Lone Star
  • Visit Google's Data & Privacy settings to manage data Google has collected

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no retention period on our end. Once you delete the app data or uninstall the app, the data is gone immediately and permanently. We have no ability to recover it.
