Privacy Policy

Lone Star Puzzles Android — Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Lone Star ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by Alex Sehlke. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

This policy applies to the Android version of Lone Star, available on Google Play.

Information We Collect

The App does not require an account, login, or registration. We do not directly collect, transmit, or share any personal data. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information as described below.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device to support gameplay:

  • Puzzle progress and completion status
  • Game settings and preferences (e.g., sound, haptics, theme)
  • Statistics such as solve times, streaks, and hint usage

This data is stored using the device's local storage (Android DataStore/SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted to any server or third party. It remains entirely on your device and can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App.

Advertising

The free version of the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address (used for approximate location and fraud prevention)
  • General device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not have access to personally identifiable information collected by AdMob.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Data from AdMob.

Google Play Games Services

The App integrates with Google Play Games Services for leaderboards. If you sign in to Play Games, Google may collect:

  • Your Play Games profile information (display name, profile picture)
  • Game activity (scores submitted to leaderboards)

Use of Google Play Games Services is optional. You can play the game without signing in. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional Pro upgrade to remove ads. Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment information — all transactions are handled by Google Play. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Network Access

All puzzles are bundled within the App and do not require an internet connection. The App connects to the internet for:

  • Loading and displaying advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Google Play Games Services (leaderboards, if signed in)
  • Google Play Billing (purchase verification)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data shared with third-party services is as described in the Advertising, Play Games, and In-App Purchases sections above, which is handled directly by Google's SDKs.

Data Retention

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store user data. You can delete all app data at any time through your device's application settings or by uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated for general audiences and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App uses Google AdMob which is configured to comply with applicable regulations, including COPPA. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
