Lone Star Puzzles — Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the game work?

In each puzzle, place stars on the grid so that every row, column, and outlined region contains exactly the required number of stars. No two stars may touch each other, not even diagonally. Tap a cell once to place a star, tap again to mark it with an X (to remind yourself a star cannot go there), and tap a third time to clear it.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All puzzles are included with the app and work fully offline. An internet connection is only used for displaying ads in the free version.

How do I reset my progress?

Open the app and go to Settings > Reset All Progress. This will clear all saved puzzle completions, statistics, and streaks. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

How do I restore my purchases?

iOS: If you previously purchased the Pro upgrade, go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your purchase will be verified with the App Store and restored automatically.

Android: Pro purchases are tied to your Google Play account and should restore automatically when you sign in with the same account. If not, go to Settings > Restore Purchases.

The app is running slowly or not responding

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure your device is running the latest version of iOS or Android and that the app is up to date. Restarting your device can also help resolve occasional performance issues.
